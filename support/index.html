Frequently Asked Questions

Does MemSweep collect or upload my data?
No. MemSweep runs entirely on your device. The app has no accounts, servers, analytics, or tracking.

Can I recover a photo after confirming delete?
Usually yes. Deleted photos move to the Photos app’s Recently Deleted album for up to 30 days.

What are Pending Deletes?
Swiping left adds a photo to Pending Deletes. Nothing is removed until you review and confirm the batch.

Does MemSweep show my entire photo library?
Yes, except when using Limited Photos access, hidden items, or iCloud items that could not be retrieved.

Can I undo a swipe?
Yes. The last 6 swipes can be undone while you remain in the session.

What happens when I swipe right?
Swipe right means keep. Behavior varies by mode, but the photo will not be marked for deletion.

Does MemSweep require internet?
No. Internet is only needed for purchases or viewing items stored in iCloud.

How do I report a problem?
Open Settings in the app and choose Report Bug, or email support@memsweep.com.

What happens if I uninstall the app?
Local MemSweep data is removed, but your photo library remains unchanged. Purchases can be restored.

Privacy

MemSweep is designed with a privacy-first architecture. The app does not run its own servers, does not track users, and does not transmit photo data off the device.
