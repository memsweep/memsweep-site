MemSweep Privacy Policy

MemSweep is designed to respect your privacy.

Data Collection

MemSweep does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

Photo Library Access

MemSweep requires permission to access your photo library in order to display and organize your photos. All processing happens on your device.

Analytics

MemSweep stores limited analytics locally on your device to improve the app experience. This information never leaves your device.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store.

Contact

For support or questions contact: support@memsweep.com
